Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

LASK is a local-first macOS app. In normal usage, your screenshots, notes, clipboard items, and other user content stay on your device.

LASK does not use analytics and does not include advertising trackers.

For licensing activation and validation, limited technical and purchase-related data may be processed to verify your license.

LASK may use Sparkle to check version and update information so you can receive app updates.

If you contact support, your email and message are used only to respond to your support request.
